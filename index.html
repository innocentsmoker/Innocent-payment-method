Secure Payment

Your payment is processed securely via Safaricom's M-Pesa STK Push system. You will receive a prompt on your phone to enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete the transaction. We never store your PIN or personal financial information.

How M-Pesa STK Push Works

1

Enter Your Details

Provide your M-Pesa registered phone number and the amount you want to pay (KES 30 - 5,000).

2

Initiate STK Push

Click "Initiate M-Pesa STK Push" to send a payment request directly to your phone via SMS.

3

Enter M-Pesa PIN

Check your phone for an M-Pesa STK Push prompt and enter your PIN to authorize the payment.

4

Payment Confirmation

You'll receive an M-Pesa confirmation message and see a success notification on this page.

About M-Pesa STK Push

STK (Sim ToolKit) Push is a Safaricom service that sends a direct payment prompt to your phone. You don't need the M-Pesa app or to dial any USSD codes. Just enter your PIN when prompted to complete payment to Pochi La Biashara.
