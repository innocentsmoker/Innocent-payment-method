Secure Payment

Your payment is processed securely through Safaricom's M-Pesa system. You will receive a prompt on your phone to enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete the transaction. We never store your PIN or personal financial information.

How It Works

1

Enter Your Details

Provide your M-Pesa registered phone number and the amount you want to pay.

2

Request Payment

Click "Request Payment" to initiate the transaction. Our system will send a payment request to your phone.

3

Enter M-Pesa PIN

Check your phone for an M-Pesa prompt and enter your PIN to authorize the payment to Pochi La Biashara (0741 212 845).

4

Payment Confirmation

You'll receive an M-Pesa confirmation message and see a success notification on this page.

About Pochi La Biashara

Pochi La Biashara is a Safaricom service that allows businesses to receive payments directly from customers via M-Pesa without needing a till number. Customers can pay by simply entering the business phone number.
